Welcome to the savage lands

The realm of reptiles is an untamed frontier ruled by grand beasts beyond humanity's most ancient imagination; Dinosaurs. Formerly known as Terrible Lizards, Realm of Reptiles is a comic series written by Adithya "Aadhira" Janish and produced by Studiocomix Press as part of the Toonix program.
